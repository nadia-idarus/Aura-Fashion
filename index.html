The Aura Philosophy

At Aura Attire, we believe in the power of simplicity. We create high-quality, timeless pieces designed to integrate seamlessly into your wardrobe. Our commitment lies in sustainable practices, meticulous craftsmanship, and enduring style over fleeting trends. Dress with intention, live with elegance.

Customer Care

We offer standard and express shipping options. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the country. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary. Please see our full shipping policy for details.

We accept returns and exchanges on unworn items with tags attached within 30 days of purchase. Please visit our Returns Portal to initiate a return or exchange. Some exclusions apply.

Finding the perfect fit is important. Please refer to our detailed Sizing Guide page, which includes measurements for all our garment types. If you need further assistance, contact our customer care team.
